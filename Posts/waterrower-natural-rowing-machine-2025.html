Full-Body Workout Benefits

The Four Phases of the Rowing Stroke

Proper rowing technique consists of four distinct phases: the catch, drive, finish, and recovery. During the drive phase, you generate approximately 60% of power from legs, 20% from core, and 20% from arms.
