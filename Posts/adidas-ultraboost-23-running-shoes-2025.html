Adidas Running Heritage and Boost Technology

Adidas introduced Boost foam technology in 2013, revolutionizing running shoe cushioning through particle foam technology that provided unprecedented energy return combined with responsive feel. Over a decade of refinement, Boost has become synonymous with premium running cushioning that balances softness with performance. The Ultraboost line specifically represents Adidas' premium Boost platform, and the 23rd generation incorporates all lessons learned from millions of miles run in previous Ultraboost versions.

Understanding Boost Foam Technology

Boost uses thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) particles bonded together in a matrix structure that provides exceptional energy return. Each Boost particle absorbs impact energy individually, then returns that energy as you push off. This creates the distinctive "bouncy" feeling that Boost is famous forâ€”runners describe it as "springy" and "energetic" even in maximum-cushion contexts. Boost maintains consistent properties through 500+ miles, ensuring your shoes feel just as responsive late in their lifespan as when brand new.
