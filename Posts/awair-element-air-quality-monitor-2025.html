Indoor air quality depends on multiple variables. Carbon dioxide (CO2) accumulates in poorly ventilated spaces, causing drowsiness and cognitive decline. Volatile organic compounds (VOCs) come from paints, furniture, cleaning products, and other materials, causing respiratory irritation and headaches.
