Introduction: The Pinnacle of Audio Engineering

These aren't just speakers; they're precision instruments that transform listening into an immersive, emotional experience.

Whether you're a critical listener building the ultimate listening room, a recording professional needing reference monitors, or simply someone who refuses to compromise on audio quality, the 800 D4 speakers represent the absolute state of the art in loudspeaker design.

Revolutionary Diamond Dome Tweeters

The heart of the 800 D4's exceptional high-frequency performance is its genuine diamond dome tweeter:

Why Diamond?

  • Incredible Stiffness: Diamond is the hardest natural material on Earth, providing unmatched rigidity that eliminates cone breakup at high frequencies.
  • Superior Speed: Diamond's extreme stiffness allows the dome to accelerate and decelerate faster than any other material, reproducing transients with perfect accuracy.
  • Extended Frequency Response: The 800 D4's diamond tweeter operates flat to 33kHz—well beyond human hearing—ensuring zero coloration in the audible range.
  • Minimal Distortion: The diamond dome's perfect pistonic motion produces less than 0.1% THD even at high output levels.
  • Temperature Stability: Diamond's exceptional thermal conductivity dissipates heat instantly, preventing compression and maintaining consistent performance.

Reverse Dome Design

  • Unique reverse-mounted configuration optimizes the acoustic center
  • Perfectly time-aligns with the midrange driver for coherent soundstage
  • Solid body aluminum housing eliminates unwanted resonances
  • Optimized tube loading provides smooth frequency extension

The Sound of Diamond

The diamond tweeter produces high frequencies with an effortlessness and purity that no metal or fabric dome can match. Cymbals shimmer with realistic decay, strings have natural texture and air, and female vocals possess ethereal presence. The complete absence of grain or harshness allows you to listen at realistic levels for hours without fatigue.

Biomimetic Suspension Midrange

The 800 D4 features Bowers & Wilkins' groundbreaking Biomimetic Suspension technology in its midrange driver:

Inspired by Nature

  • Spider Web Geometry: The suspension uses a radial pattern inspired by spider webs, providing optimal stiffness-to-weight ratio.
  • Perfect Linearity: Maintains linear excursion across the entire operating range for zero distortion.
  • Reduced Mass: Lighter suspension allows the driver to respond faster to transient signals.
  • Controlled Breakup: Carefully engineered to push breakup modes beyond the driver's operating range.

Continuum Cone Material

  • Woven Composite: Proprietary Continuum material combines exceptional rigidity with ideal internal damping.
  • Optimal Thickness: Precision-controlled thickness ensures perfect balance of stiffness and mass.
  • Natural Sound: The cone material's characteristics produce remarkably natural, uncolored midrange.
  • 6-inch Diameter: Carefully sized for optimal coverage pattern and integration with tweeter and woofers.

Turbine Head Design

  • Distinctive turbine-shaped enclosure eliminates parallel surfaces
  • Decoupled mounting isolates midrange from cabinet vibrations
  • Acoustically transparent grille maintains performance when covered
  • Precision CNC-machined from solid aluminum billet

Advanced Bass System

The 800 D4 employs sophisticated bass driver technology for deep, accurate low frequencies:

Aerofoil Cone Profile

  • Two 10-inch Woofers: Dual bass drivers provide massive radiating area for effortless low-frequency output.
  • Carbon Fiber Construction: Aerospace-grade carbon fiber cones are extremely rigid yet remarkably light.
  • Aerofoil Shaping: Unique cone profile inspired by aircraft wings minimizes air turbulence and reduces distortion.
  • Extended Excursion: Long-throw voice coils allow deep bass extension without compression.

Reactance Annulling Bass Alignment

  • Proprietary bass alignment optimizes enclosure volume and port tuning
  • Eliminates impedance irregularities for easier amplifier load
  • Provides ruler-flat response down to 14Hz in-room
  • Maintains perfect control and articulation even at high output levels

Flowport Technology

  • Dimpled Surface: Golf ball-like texture on ports reduces air turbulence by up to 60%.
  • Longer Ports: Extended port length allows lower tuning frequency for deeper bass.
  • Reduced Chuffing: Smooth airflow eliminates port noise even at high SPLs.
  • Optimized Placement: Ports positioned to minimize interaction with room boundaries.

Cabinet Construction and Design

The 800 D4's enclosure represents the pinnacle of loudspeaker cabinet engineering:

Matrix Bracing

  • Honeycomb Structure: Internal matrix bracing creates a rigid honeycomb that eliminates cabinet resonances.
  • Strategic Placement: Computer-optimized bracing targets specific resonant modes.
  • Multi-Layer MDF: Premium multi-density fiberboard provides ideal combination of mass and damping.
  • Measured Silence: Cabinet walls remain measurably inert even at high SPLs—the sound comes purely from the drivers.

Reverse Wrap Cabinet

  • Continuous curved back panel wraps around to the front
  • Eliminates sharp corners and parallel surfaces that cause standing waves
  • Improves structural rigidity while reducing internal reflections
  • Distinctive aesthetic that's both beautiful and functional

Premium Finishes

  • Piano Black Gloss: Hand-polished to mirror finish with multiple lacquer coats.
  • Satin White: Elegant matte white finish for contemporary interiors.
  • Real Wood Veneers: Available in Santos Rosewood and Rosenut with hand-matched grain.
  • Two-Tone Options: Combine finishes (e.g., gloss black body with wood turbine head) for custom look.
  • Custom Colors: Special order finishes available through authorized dealers.

Adjustable Spike System

  • Heavy-duty outrigger feet with precision-machined spikes
  • Independent adjustment for each spike for perfect leveling
  • Couples speaker firmly to floor for optimal bass articulation
  • Optional rubber footers for hardwood floor protection

Crossover Network Excellence

The 800 D4's crossover represents state-of-the-art passive network design:

  • Precision Components: Audiophile-grade capacitors, inductors, and resistors selected for sonic transparency.
  • Fourth-Order Slopes: Steep 24dB/octave slopes ensure optimal driver integration and power handling.
  • Phase Coherent: Carefully engineered to maintain phase coherence across the entire frequency spectrum.
  • Bi-Wire Capable: Separate terminals for high and low frequencies allow bi-wiring or bi-amping.
  • Minimal Signal Path: Shortest possible connection paths preserve signal integrity.
  • Premium Terminals: WBT NextGen binding posts accept spades, bananas, or bare wire up to 10AWG.

Setup and Room Considerations

The 800 D4 speakers demand careful setup to achieve their full potential:

Room Requirements

  • Minimum Size: Ideally 16' x 20' or larger—these speakers need room to breathe.
  • Ceiling Height: 8-foot minimum; 9-10 feet ideal for optimal bass loading.
  • Acoustic Treatment: Proper room treatment maximizes the speakers' potential.
  • Power Requirements: Dedicated 20-amp circuit recommended for high-power amplification.

Placement Guidelines

  • Distance from Walls: Minimum 3-4 feet from rear wall; 2-3 feet from side walls.
  • Spacing: 8-10 feet apart for optimal soundstage width.
  • Toe-In: Typically 10-20 degrees aimed at listening position.
  • Listening Distance: 1.5x to 2.5x the speaker spacing for ideal imaging.
  • Professional Setup: Authorized dealers provide professional calibration services.

Amplification Requirements

  • Minimum Power: 100 watts per channel minimum; 200+ watts recommended.
  • High Current Capability: Amplifiers must deliver high current into 3-4 ohm loads.
  • Quality Over Quantity: High-quality 100W amplifier better than mediocre 500W amp.
  • Recommended Brands: McIntosh, Audio Research, Pass Labs, D'Agostino, Boulder, Gryphon.
  • Total System Cost: Plan to spend at least as much on amplification as on the speakers.

Break-In Period

The 800 D4 speakers require substantial break-in time to reach full performance:

  • Expect 100-200 hours before drivers fully settle
  • Bass will deepen and tighten as woofer surrounds loosen
  • Midrange will open up and become more transparent
  • Diamond tweeter changes little but crossover components stabilize
  • Play varied musical material at moderate-to-loud levels for optimal break-in

You're getting the same reference that professionals use to create the music you love.
