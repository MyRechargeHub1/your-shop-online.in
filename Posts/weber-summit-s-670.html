Innovative Flavorizer Bars

Weber's patented Flavorizer bars sit between burners and cooking grates, catching drippings that vaporize into flavorful smoke. Flare-Up Prevention

The angled Flavorizer bars channel grease away from burner ports while vaporizing it into flavor-enhancing smoke. This dramatically reduces the dangerous flare-ups that char food and cause frustration on lesser grills. The result is better-tasting food and safer, more controlled cooking.
