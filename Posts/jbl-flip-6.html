Frequently Asked Questions

Can the JBL Flip 6 float in water?

No, the Flip 6 is waterproof but does not float. If dropped in water, it will sink. Some users place it on pool floats or in mesh bags for pool use.

Does it have a speakerphone for calls?

No, the Flip 6 removed the speakerphone feature that was present in the Flip 5. It's designed purely for music playback.

Can I use it while charging?

Yes, you can play music while the speaker charges. Note that it's not waterproof while charging port is open.

What's the difference between Flip 5 and Flip 6?

Flip 6 improvements include: better sound quality with separate tweeter and woofer, louder volume, IP67 rating (vs. IPX7), USB-C charging, and PartyBoost (replacing Connect+). The Flip 5 had speakerphone capability which Flip 6 removed.

Can I connect it to my TV?

If your TV has Bluetooth output, yes. However, there may be noticeable audio delay during video playback. Bluetooth latency makes it less ideal for TV use compared to wired or dedicated TV speakers.
