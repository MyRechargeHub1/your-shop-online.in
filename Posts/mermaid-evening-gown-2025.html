Neckline and Bodice Design Options

Care and Maintenance

Quality mermaid gowns deserve proper care to maintain their beauty and construction integrity.

Professional Cleaning

Always have mermaid gowns professionally cleaned by specialists experienced with delicate formal wear. Specify the form-fitting nature of the dress and any embellishments so appropriate cleaning methods are used. Professional cleaning preserves fabric integrity and prevents damage.

Storage and Preservation

Store mermaid gowns on padded hangers in breathable garment bags. The fitted construction should be supported by the hanger to maintain shape. Never fold form-fitting gowns, as the weight can create permanent creasing. Keep in climate-controlled storage away from direct sunlight.
