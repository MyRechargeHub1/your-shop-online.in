Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a subscription to use Roku?

No! Roku is free to use. You only pay for individual streaming services you subscribe to (Netflix, Disney+, etc.). The Roku Channel and many others offer free content with no subscription required.

Will this work with my TV?

Yes, any TV with an HDMI port works with Roku. For 4K HDR, you'll need a 4K TV with HDMI 2.0 or newer. The Roku works with all TV brands: Samsung, LG, Sony, Vizio, TCL, and more.

Can I use Roku without internet?

No, Roku requires an internet connection to stream content. You can download content for offline viewing in some apps (like Netflix) on mobile devices, but not directly to Roku.

How much internet speed do I need for 4K streaming?

Roku recommends 25 Mbps for 4K content. In testing, 4K streams worked reliably at 15-20 Mbps, though 25+ Mbps ensures buffer-free streaming even with multiple devices using WiFi.

Can I control Roku with Alexa or Google Assistant?

Yes! Enable the Roku skill for Alexa or Google Assistant to control your Roku with voice commands through smart speakers or smart displays.
