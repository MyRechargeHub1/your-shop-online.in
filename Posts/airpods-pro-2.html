Apple AirPods Pro 2: Premium Active Noise Cancellation

November 24, 2024 10 min read Audio

The Apple AirPods Pro 2 represent the pinnacle of wireless earbud technology in 2024. With groundbreaking improvements in Active Noise Cancellation, Adaptive Transparency, and personalized spatial audio, these earbuds set a new standard for what's possible in truly wireless audio.

Evolution of Excellence

When Apple introduced the original AirPods Pro in 2019, they revolutionized the wireless earbud market with exceptional noise cancellation and seamless integration with the Apple ecosystem. The AirPods Pro 2, released in late 2022 and continuously improved through software updates, builds on that foundation with substantial improvements across every aspect of the listening experience.

What makes the AirPods Pro 2 special isn't just one killer feature - it's the holistic approach Apple has taken to create earbuds that excel in every scenario, from commuting and working out to critical listening and phone calls.

Design and Build Quality

At first glance, the AirPods Pro 2 look nearly identical to their predecessors. Apple clearly subscribes to the "if it ain't broke, don't fix it" philosophy when it comes to the physical design. The familiar short stem design has become iconic, instantly recognizable as AirPods.

However, subtle improvements abound. The earbuds themselves are IPX4 rated for sweat and water resistance, making them suitable for workouts and light rain. The charging case has also received the same IPX4 rating, addressing a common complaint about the original model.

Comfort and Fit

Comfort is paramount for earbuds you'll wear for hours at a time, and Apple has nailed it. The AirPods Pro 2 come with four sizes of silicone tips (XS, S, M, L) - an increase from the three sizes included with the original Pro. This ensures a secure, comfortable fit for nearly every ear shape.

The ear tip fit test, accessible through iOS settings, uses the internal microphones to determine if you've selected the optimal tip size. This feature is more accurate in the second generation, helping you achieve the best possible seal for maximum noise cancellation and audio quality.

Unlike some competing earbuds that create pressure buildup, the AirPods Pro 2's vented design equalizes pressure, allowing for comfortable all-day wear without fatigue.

The H2 Chip: The Brain Behind the Magic

The custom-designed Apple H2 chip is the heart of the AirPods Pro 2, enabling capabilities that weren't possible with the H1 chip in the original model. This isn't just an incremental upgrade - the H2 represents a fundamental leap in what wireless earbuds can achieve.

The H2 chip handles computational audio in real-time, processing millions of audio samples per second to deliver features like Adaptive Audio, Personalized Spatial Audio, and significantly improved Active Noise Cancellation. The increased processing power also improves connection stability and reduces audio latency.

Active Noise Cancellation: Best in Class

Apple claims up to 2x better Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) compared to the original AirPods Pro, and real-world use confirms this isn't marketing hyperbole. The AirPods Pro 2 deliver industry-leading noise cancellation that rivals or exceeds larger over-ear headphones.

How It Works

The ANC system uses six microphones - three outward-facing and three inward-facing - to continuously sample environmental sound. The H2 chip then generates precise anti-noise that cancels unwanted sound before it reaches your ears. This happens 48,000 times per second, adapting in real-time to changes in your environment and fit.

The result is remarkable. Airplane cabin noise virtually disappears. The rumble of subway trains fades away. Office chatter becomes barely noticeable. You can finally hear your music or podcasts clearly in noisy environments without having to crank the volume to unsafe levels.

Real-World Performance

On airplanes, the AirPods Pro 2 excel at eliminating the constant drone of engine noise. While voices and sudden sounds aren't completely eliminated (no ANC system can do that), the reduction is substantial enough that you can enjoy content at comfortable volumes throughout your flight.

For commuters, these earbuds transform public transportation. The constant rumble of trains and buses fades into the background, creating a personal bubble of calm in chaotic environments. Even in open-plan offices with constant activity, the ANC enables focus when you need it most.

Adaptive Transparency: Hear What Matters

Transparency mode allows external sound to pass through while you're wearing the earbuds, letting you remain aware of your surroundings without removing them. The AirPods Pro 2's Adaptive Transparency takes this concept to the next level.

The H2 chip continuously monitors environmental noise and automatically reduces loud sounds like construction equipment, passing vehicles, or sirens to a more comfortable level. Importantly, it does this in real-time and without affecting normal sounds like conversations, footsteps, or nature sounds.

This makes Adaptive Transparency genuinely useful in ways that traditional transparency modes aren't. You can walk down busy city streets, have conversations, and remain aware of your environment while still enjoying your music - all without the jarring experience of sudden loud noises.

Spatial Audio: Immersive Sound

Personalized Spatial Audio is one of the AirPods Pro 2's most impressive features. Using the TrueDepth camera on your iPhone or iPad, the system creates a personalized profile of your unique ear shape. This allows it to precisely tune spatial audio to deliver sound that seems to come from all around you, not just from your earbuds.

Dynamic Head Tracking

Gyroscopes and accelerometers in each earbud track your head movements, adjusting the audio so it remains anchored to your device or, for supported content, to your physical space. Watch a movie on your iPad, and the dialogue stays centered on the screen even as you turn your head. The effect is subtle but remarkably convincing.

For music, spatial audio creates a wider soundstage than traditional stereo, making instruments feel more separated and present. Not all music is mixed for spatial audio, but when it is, the difference is striking.

Sound Quality: Audiophile-Worthy Performance

Previous AirPods models were occasionally criticized by audiophiles for prioritizing convenience over absolute sound quality. The AirPods Pro 2 largely put those concerns to rest, delivering sound quality that stands up to scrutiny from even discerning listeners.

Audio Characteristics

The frequency response is well-balanced, avoiding the bass-heavy tuning common in many consumer earbuds. Bass is present and impactful without overwhelming the mids and highs. Vocal reproduction is clear and natural. Treble is detailed without becoming harsh or fatiguing.

The custom high-excursion driver and amplifier work in concert to deliver surprisingly powerful sound from such small drivers. Maximum volume levels are impressive while remaining distortion-free, and the dynamic range handles everything from whisper-quiet passages to explosive crescendos with ease.

Adaptive EQ

The inward-facing microphones measure what you're hearing, allowing the H2 chip to adjust frequencies in real-time to account for your unique ear shape and the seal quality. This Adaptive EQ ensures consistent sound quality regardless of fit variations, and it happens automatically without any user intervention.

Call Quality: Crystal Clear Communication

For many users, call quality is just as important as music playback, and the AirPods Pro 2 excel here. The improved microphone array, combined with advanced voice isolation algorithms, ensures your voice comes through clearly even in noisy environments.

The voice-accelerometer detects vibrations from your jawbone when you speak, helping to distinguish your voice from background noise. Combined with the microphone array's beamforming capabilities, the result is exceptional call quality that rivals dedicated Bluetooth headsets.

Video calls on Zoom, Teams, or FaceTime benefit from the same technology, making the AirPods Pro 2 an excellent choice for remote work scenarios. Colleagues will hear you clearly without the distracting background noise that plagues so many wireless earbuds.

Battery Life: All-Day Performance

Battery life sees welcome improvements over the original AirPods Pro. With ANC enabled, you'll get up to 6 hours of listening time per charge - a full hour more than the first generation. With ANC off, that extends even further.

The charging case provides an additional 24 hours of listening time, bringing total battery life to 30 hours. For most users, this means charging the case once or twice per week rather than daily.

Charging Options

The case supports multiple charging methods: Lightning cable, MagSafe, Qi wireless charging, and even Apple Watch chargers. This flexibility means you can top up using whatever charging solution you have available.

Fast charging provides about an hour of listening time from just five minutes in the case, perfect for quick top-ups before leaving the house. A full case charge takes approximately an hour via Lightning cable.

The Case: Small but Feature-Rich

The charging case deserves special mention for its impressive feature set. Beyond just charging, it includes several thoughtful additions:

  • Built-in Speaker: Plays sounds to help locate the case via Find My, and provides confirmation tones for pairing and low battery warnings.
  • Lanyard Loop: A small loop on the side allows you to attach a strap (sold separately) for easier carrying.
  • Precision Finding: U1 chip integration enables directional finding on iPhone 11 and later, showing you exactly where your case is located.
  • IPX4 Rating: The case itself is now water-resistant, protecting your investment from spills and splashes.

Apple Ecosystem Integration

One of the AirPods Pro 2's greatest strengths is seamless integration with the Apple ecosystem. If you own multiple Apple devices, the experience is magical.

Automatic Switching

The earbuds intelligently switch between your devices based on what you're doing. Start watching a video on your iPad, and audio automatically switches from your iPhone. Answer a phone call, and it switches back. The transitions are nearly instantaneous and completely automatic.

Audio Sharing

Share audio with a friend who also has AirPods or Beats headphones. Both of you can listen to the same content simultaneously from one device, perfect for watching movies or listening to music together.

Siri Integration

Just say "Hey Siri" to invoke Apple's assistant without touching your device. Control music playback, make calls, get directions, and more using just your voice. The microphones ensure Siri hears you clearly even in noisy environments.

Touch Controls and Gestures

The stems of the AirPods Pro 2 house force sensors that register presses. The controls are intuitive once learned:

  • Single press: Play/pause, answer/end calls
  • Double press: Skip forward
  • Triple press: Skip backward
  • Long press: Switch between ANC and Transparency mode
  • Swipe (on supported models): Volume control

The new volume swipe gesture, introduced in a software update, allows you to adjust volume by swiping up or down on the stem. This provides tactile control without needing to reach for your device - a small but significant convenience improvement.

Find My Integration

Lost earbuds are frustrating, but the AirPods Pro 2's Find My integration minimizes that concern. You can view their last known location on a map, play a sound from either earbud or the case, and use Precision Finding to locate them with step-by-step directions.

The case's built-in speaker makes locating it much easier than the silent case of the original model. Even if your earbuds are in the case, you can trigger a sound to help find them.

Hearing Health Features

Apple takes hearing health seriously, and the AirPods Pro 2 include several features to protect your ears:

  • Headphone Accommodations: Amplify soft sounds and adjust frequencies to suit your hearing needs
  • Conversation Boost: Enhance voices in front of you to make conversations easier to follow
  • Background Sounds: Play ambient sounds like rain or ocean waves to mask environmental noise and help you focus or sleep
  • Volume Limit Notifications: Alerts you if listening at high volumes for extended periods

Accessibility Features

The AirPods Pro 2 include thoughtful accessibility features that make them usable for people with various needs:

  • Live Listen turns your iPhone into a directional microphone
  • Mono Audio combines left and right channels for users with hearing in one ear
  • Announce Notifications reads incoming notifications aloud
  • Announce Calls lets you know who's calling without looking at your phone

Software Updates and Future-Proofing

Apple regularly updates AirPods Pro firmware, adding new features and improvements over time. Recent updates have added Adaptive Audio, Personalized Volume, and Conversation Awareness - features that didn't exist at launch.

This commitment to ongoing improvement means your AirPods Pro 2 will gain capabilities over time rather than becoming obsolete. Apple's track record suggests these earbuds will receive meaningful updates for years to come.

Comparison to Competitors

The wireless earbud market is crowded with excellent options, but the AirPods Pro 2 stand out in several ways:

  • Better ANC than Sony WF-1000XM5 in many scenarios
  • Superior ecosystem integration compared to Samsung Galaxy Buds2 Pro
  • More reliable connection than most competitors
  • Better call quality than Bose QuietComfort Earbuds II

The main disadvantage is price and the requirement for Apple devices to unlock all features. For Android users, other options may provide better value.

Who Should Buy the AirPods Pro 2?

Perfect For:

  • iPhone Users: The seamless integration makes these the obvious choice for Apple device owners
  • Commuters: Industry-leading ANC makes travel more pleasant
  • Fitness Enthusiasts: IPX4 rating and secure fit work well for workouts
  • Remote Workers: Exceptional call quality enhances virtual meetings
  • Audiophiles: Sound quality is genuinely impressive

Consider Alternatives If:

  • You primarily use Android devices (features are limited)
  • You need extremely long battery life (some competitors offer 8+ hours)
  • Budget is a primary concern (excellent cheaper options exist)
  • You prefer over-ear headphones for ultimate ANC performance

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Industry-leading Active Noise Cancellation
  • Innovative Adaptive Transparency mode
  • Personalized Spatial Audio with head tracking
  • Excellent sound quality with balanced tuning
  • Outstanding call quality
  • Improved battery life (6 hours + 24 with case)
  • IPX4 water resistance for buds and case
  • Seamless integration with Apple ecosystem
  • Regular software updates with new features
  • Comprehensive Find My integration
  • Comfortable for extended wear
  • Premium build quality

Cons:

  • Premium price point
  • Best features require Apple devices
  • No support for high-quality codecs like LDAC or aptX
  • Lightning charging (not USB-C yet)
  • Limited customization compared to some competitors

Final Verdict

The Apple AirPods Pro 2 are the best wireless earbuds you can buy in 2024 if you're in the Apple ecosystem. They excel in every meaningful category: noise cancellation, sound quality, comfort, battery life, and ecosystem integration.

The premium price is justified by the exceptional experience they deliver. While cheaper alternatives exist, none match the holistic excellence of the AirPods Pro 2. The combination of H2-powered computational audio, industry-leading ANC, personalized spatial audio, and seamless Apple integration creates an experience that's greater than the sum of its impressive parts.

For iPhone users, these earbuds are an easy recommendation. They transform daily activities like commuting, working out, and taking calls into more pleasant experiences. The continuous improvements through software updates ensure they'll remain excellent for years to come.
