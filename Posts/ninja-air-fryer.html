Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to use oil with the Ninja Air Fryer?

Most foods benefit from a light coating of oil (spray or brush), but fatty foods like chicken wings don't require any added oil. Using 1-2 teaspoons of oil enhances browning and flavor.

Can I cook frozen food without thawing?

Absolutely! The air fryer excels at cooking frozen foods like french fries, chicken nuggets, fish sticks, and mozzarella sticks directly from frozen. Add 2-3 minutes to standard cooking times.

How loud is the Ninja Air Fryer?

The fan creates white noise similar to a hair dryer at about 65 decibels—noticeable but not disruptive. You can hold conversations while it operates.

Can I bake in the air fryer?

Yes! The air fryer works great for baking muffins, cookies, and even small cakes. Use oven-safe baking dishes that fit inside the basket.

Is it safe to use aluminum foil or parchment paper?

Yes, both are safe to use. Ensure foil/parchment doesn't block air flow and is weighted down by food. Use perforated parchment liners designed for air fryers for best results.
