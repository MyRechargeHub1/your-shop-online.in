The Commercial series uses a bowl-lift mechanism rather than the tilt-head design of smaller KitchenAid models. This isn't arbitraryâ€”it's engineering necessity for handling the weight and stress of large batches.

How Bowl-Lift Works

Two lever arms on either side of the mixer support the bowl, raising and lowering it to engage or clear the attachment. The bowl locks securely into position during mixing, preventing any movement or wobble even with heavy, stiff doughs.
