Infrared Griddle and Charbroiler

  • Integrated Griddle: Optional infrared griddle section for pancakes, eggs, and fajitas.

Continuous Grates

Installation and Requirements

The Wolf 48" range requires professional installation and proper infrastructure:

Installation Specifications

  • Width: 48 inches (requires 48" cabinet opening)
  • Depth: 27 inches (standard counter depth)
  • Height: 36 inches to cooking surface (standard counter height)
  • Weight: Approximately 600-700 lbs (requires reinforced floor)

Utility Requirements

  • Gas Supply: Natural gas or propane (conversion kit available)

  • Electrical: Dedicated 240V, 50-amp circuit for ovens

  • Professional Installation: Factory-authorized installer required to maintain warranty

Ventilation Considerations

  • Wolf recommends 600-1200 CFM hood for 48" range
  • Make-up air may be required by code in some jurisdictions
  • Proper ventilation ensures comfortable cooking environment
  • Hood should extend 3" beyond range on each side for optimal capture

Maintenance and Cleaning

Keeping the Wolf range in top condition requires regular maintenance:

Daily Cleaning

  • Cooktop: Wipe stainless surface with damp cloth and dry thoroughly.
  • Grates: Remove and hand wash or place in dishwasher (if porcelain-coated).
  • Burner Caps: Wipe clean and ensure proper seating for optimal flame.
  • Control Knobs: Wipe with damp cloth; remove for deeper cleaning if needed.

Periodic Maintenance

  • Burner Ports: Use soft brush to clear any blockages monthly.
  • Oven Interior: Run self-clean cycle every 3-6 months depending on use.
  • Door Gaskets: Inspect seals annually; replace if damaged or worn.
  • Hinges: Lubricate oven door hinges annually for smooth operation.

Professional Service

  • Annual professional inspection recommended
  • Gas connections should be checked for leaks periodically
  • Calibration of oven temperatures can be adjusted if needed
  • Wolf's service network provides factory-trained technicians nationwide
