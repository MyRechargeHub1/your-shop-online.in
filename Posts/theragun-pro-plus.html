Biometric Sensor: Guided Treatment Intelligence

The PRO Plus's breakthrough feature is its built-in biometric sensor that measures heart rate variability (HRV) through your grip on the handle. This real-time physiological feedback informs optimal treatment duration and intensity.

How It Works

Hold the device naturally, and embedded sensors detect minute changes in blood flow and nervous system activity. The Therabody app displays your current stress state and recovery readiness, then recommends treatment protocols tailored to your body's current condition.

High stress reading? The app suggests longer, lower-intensity sessions focused on relaxation. Recovered and ready for performance? It recommends shorter, higher-intensity activation routines. This personalized guidance eliminates guesswork, ensuring every treatment session delivers maximum benefit.

Rotating Arm: Ergonomic Excellence

The patented rotating arm pivots to four positions, allowing you to reach every muscle group comfortably without contorting your body. Treat your back, shoulders, legs, and feet with natural arm positioning that eliminates strain.

Four Ergonomic Positions

The arm rotates in 90-degree increments, optimizing angles for different muscle groups. The neutral position works for legs and arms. Rotate 90 degrees for easy shoulder and upper back access. Five Speed Settings: Precision Control

Variable speed control from 1,750 to 2,400 percussions per minute (PPM) lets you customize treatment intensity for different muscle groups, recovery stages, and comfort levels.

  • 1,750 PPM: Gentle warm-up and cool-down
  • 1,900 PPM: Active recovery and sensitive areas
  • 2,100 PPM: General muscle maintenance

OLED screen displays current speed, battery life, and force indicator, providing clear visual feedback during use.

Six Professional Attachments

Six interchangeable attachment heads target specific muscle groups and treatment needs, each precision-engineered from medical-grade materials.

Standard Ball

Versatile all-purpose attachment for large and small muscle groups. Ideal for quads, hamstrings, glutes, and back muscles. Most users spend 80% of treatment time with this attachment.

Dampener

Softest attachment with maximum surface area for tender or bony areas. Perfect for feet, shins, and areas recovering from injury where gentler treatment is needed.

Thumb

Precision-pointed design for trigger points, lower back, hands, and feet. Concentrates force for focused treatment of knots and adhesions.

Wedge

Flat edge design for shoulder blades, IT bands, and large muscle groups. The broad contact area distributes force for comfortable sustained treatment.

Cone

Narrow tip for pinpoint precision on hands, feet, and specific trigger points. Professional therapists use this for detailed muscular work.

Supersoft

Ultra-gentle foam attachment for sensitive areas or recent injuries. Provides minimal intensity for early-stage recovery or areas with acute sensitivity.

Comparison with Competitors

vs. Hyperice Hypervolt 2 Pro ($399)

Hyperice offers quieter operation (60 dB vs 65 dB) and pressure sensor feedback. However, Theragun delivers deeper amplitude (16mm vs 14mm), more force (60 lbs vs 55 lbs), and longer battery (150 min vs 180 min). Theragun's rotating arm is also more versatile. PRO Plus costs $200 more but delivers professional-grade performance that justifies the premium.

vs. Theragun PRO (4th Gen) ($399)

The standard PRO offers identical force, amplitude, and attachments but lacks the biometric sensor and has 120-minute battery vs 150-minute. If you don't need heart rate monitoring, save $200 with the standard PRO. The Plus is worth the premium for serious athletes who value data-driven recovery optimization.

vs. Budget Massage Guns ($80-150)

Consumer-grade devices from generic brands cost 75% less but offer significantly less amplitude (10-12mm), lower force (30-40 lbs), and motors that stall under pressure. They're adequate for light recovery but can't deliver clinical-depth treatment. For serious athletes or those with chronic muscle tension, the PRO Plus's performance gap justifies the investment.
