Six Specialized Attachment Heads

The PRO Plus includes six professional-grade attachments, each designed for specific muscle groups and treatment techniques. These medical-grade closed-cell foam attachments resist bacteria and wipe clean easily.

Attachment Descriptions

Standard Ball: The versatile all-purpose attachment suitable for major muscle groups including quads, hamstrings, glutes, and back. This attachment serves as the starting point for most treatments.

Dampener: The softest attachment provides gentle treatment for sensitive areas, tender spots, or individuals new to percussive therapy. Perfect for areas near bones or joints where the standard ball feels too intense.

Thumb: Designed for trigger point therapy, this attachment targets specific knots and adhesions with precision. Use it on stubborn areas like the IT band, hip flexors, or trapezius muscles.

Cone: The pointed cone attachment reaches small, precise areas like feet, hands, and around scapulas. Excellent for targeted treatment of specific pain points.

Wedge: Ideal for shoulder blades, IT bands, and larger flat muscles. The wedge's shape enables scraping motions that release fascial restrictions.

Supersoft: The gentlest attachment perfect for ultra-sensitive areas, post-injury treatment (with physician approval), or individuals with low pain tolerance.

Science-Backed Recovery Benefits

Percussive therapy provides numerous scientifically-documented benefits for athletes and active individuals:

Enhanced Blood Flow

The rapid percussions increase blood flow to treated muscles, delivering oxygen and nutrients while removing metabolic waste products. This enhanced circulation accelerates recovery, reduces inflammation, and promotes healing.

Myofascial Release

The deep amplitude breaks up fascial adhesions and scar tissue that restrict movement and cause pain. Regular treatment maintains fascial health, improving flexibility and range of motion.

Reduced Muscle Soreness

Pre-workout percussion activates muscles and increases blood flow, improving performance and reducing injury risk. Post-workout treatment reduces delayed-onset muscle soreness (DOMS), enabling faster recovery and more frequent training.

Pain Relief

Percussive therapy provides natural pain relief through several mechanisms: blocking pain signals (gate control theory), releasing endorphins, reducing muscle spasm, and addressing underlying tension causing pain.
