Maintenance: Simple and Straightforward

The Roomba 694 requires minimal maintenance to keep running optimally.

Dustbin Cleaning

Empty the 0.3-liter dustbin after each use (or when the app notifies you). Removal is simple—press the release button, lift out the bin, and dump contents in the trash. Rinse the bin periodically for thorough cleaning.

Brush Cleaning

Remove and clean brushes weekly if you have pets, monthly otherwise. The rubber extractors resist hair tangling but benefit from occasional cleaning. The included maintenance tool removes any wrapped hair easily.

Filter Replacement

Replace the filter every 2-3 months for optimal performance. Replacement filters are inexpensive and available on Amazon.

Sensor Cleaning

Wipe cliff detection sensors and bumper sensors monthly with a soft, dry cloth. Clean sensors ensure proper navigation and stair detection.

Wheel Maintenance

Remove the front caster wheel periodically to clear accumulated hair and debris. The side wheels rarely need attention.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the Roomba 694 work on dark or black floors?

Yes, the Roomba 694's cliff sensors work on dark floors. However, extremely dark or highly reflective floors can occasionally confuse cliff sensors. Most users report no issues with dark hardwood or tile.

Can it clean multiple rooms?

Yes, the Roomba navigates through doorways to clean multiple rooms on a single charge. It doesn't create maps or clean room-by-room systematically (those features require higher-end models), but it will eventually cover all accessible areas.

How loud is the Roomba 694?

At approximately 65 decibels, it's quieter than traditional vacuums (70-80 dB) but not silent. You can hold conversations in the same room, though you'll need to raise your voice slightly. It's quiet enough for scheduled cleaning while working from home.

Does it work with 5GHz WiFi?

No, the Roomba 694 requires 2.4 GHz WiFi. Most modern routers broadcast both frequencies, so simply connect to your 2.4 GHz network during setup.

Can I use it if I have pets?

Absolutely! The Roomba 694 excels at pet hair pickup. The rubber brushes resist tangling with pet hair better than bristle brushes. Many pet owners run their Roomba daily to manage shedding.
