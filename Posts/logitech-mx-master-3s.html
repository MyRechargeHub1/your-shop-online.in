Evolution of Excellence

Logitech's MX Master series has dominated the productivity mouse category for years, and the MX Master 3S refines this winning formula to near-perfection. Building on the groundbreaking MX Master 3, the 3S addresses the few remaining criticisms while maintaining everything users loved about its predecessor.

The result is a mouse that feels like a natural extension of your hand, enhancing productivity through thoughtful design rather than gimmicky features. Every button, scroll wheel, and ergonomic curve serves a practical purpose for professionals who spend hours daily manipulating digital content.

Silent Click Technology

The most significant upgrade in the MX Master 3S is the implementation of Logitech's Quiet Clicks technology, reducing click noise by 90% compared to the MX Master 3. The silent switches transform the user experience, especially in quiet environments like offices, libraries, or homes where others are present.

Tactile Feedback Without Noise

Despite dramatically reduced noise, the clicks maintain satisfying tactile feedback. The switches feel precise and responsive, with clear actuation points that provide confidence in your clicks without the disruptive clicking sound. This achievement demonstrates sophisticated engineering â€“ it's far easier to make quiet switches feel mushy than to maintain precision while eliminating noise.

For users in shared workspaces, video calls, or quiet home offices, the silent operation is transformative. You can work late without disturbing sleeping family members, take calls without click noise bleeding into your microphone, or work in shared offices without annoying colleagues.

Durability Rating

The silent switches are rated for 10 million clicks â€“ identical to standard switches. This longevity rating suggests the mouse will outlast years of professional use, making it a long-term investment rather than a disposable peripheral.

Upgraded 8K DPI Sensor

The MX Master 3S features an upgraded 8,000 DPI sensor (up from 4,000 DPI in the MX Master 3) that delivers exceptional precision across various surfaces. While most users won't operate at maximum DPI, the upgraded sensor provides noticeably improved tracking accuracy at all sensitivity levels.

Darkfield Tracking Technology

Logitech's Darkfield laser tracking works on virtually any surface, including glass â€“ a capability that eludes most optical mice. Whether you're working on a wooden desk, glass table, granite countertop, or even directly on fabric, the MX Master 3S tracks flawlessly.

This surface versatility means you're never hunting for a mousepad or dealing with tracking issues when working in different locations. The freedom to work anywhere without surface constraints enhances flexibility for professionals who move between workspaces.

Adjustable Sensitivity

Through the Logi Options+ software, you can adjust tracking speed and create application-specific DPI settings. Set lower sensitivity for precision work in photo editing or design applications, higher sensitivity for general navigation, and create custom profiles that switch automatically based on active software.

MagSpeed Electromagnetic Scrolling

The MagSpeed electromagnetic scroll wheel remains one of the MX Master 3S's most distinctive and beloved features. Unlike traditional ratcheted scroll wheels, MagSpeed uses magnets for precision scrolling with the option to shift to free-spin mode for flying through long documents.

Precision Ratchet Mode

In ratchet mode, the scroll wheel provides tactile detents that make precise, controlled scrolling effortless. Each notch gives satisfying feedback, allowing accurate scrolling through menus, lists, or spreadsheets. The resistance is perfectly balanced â€“ firm enough for control but light enough to prevent finger fatigue.

Free-Spin Mode

A gentle flick shifts the wheel into free-spin mode, where it spins with minimal friction like a precision flywheel. A single flick scrolls through hundreds of pages in seconds â€“ invaluable for navigating lengthy documents, long web pages, or extensive spreadsheets.

The transition between modes is seamless and intuitive. The wheel intelligently shifts based on scroll velocity, or you can manually toggle modes using a dedicated button. This adaptability makes the MagSpeed wheel feel natural and responsive rather than requiring conscious mode switching.

Horizontal Scroll Wheel

A unique thumb-operated horizontal scroll wheel enables side-scrolling without keyboard shortcuts or awkward mouse gymnastics. This feature is particularly valuable for wide spreadsheets, timeline-based applications, or any horizontal content navigation.

The horizontal wheel operates quietly and smoothly, positioned perfectly for thumb access without interfering with grip. Like the main scroll wheel, it provides tactile feedback that makes blind operation natural and accurate.

Ergonomic Excellence

The MX Master 3S continues the series' tradition of exceptional ergonomic design, sculpted to support your hand naturally during extended use.

Hand-Sculpted Contours

The mouse's shape follows the natural resting position of a right hand (note: this mouse is right-handed only), with a pronounced thumb rest, sculpted finger grooves, and palm support that cradles your hand without requiring gripping force. This passive support dramatically reduces hand and wrist fatigue during marathon work sessions.

The thumb rest is particularly well-designed, featuring a textured rubber surface that provides secure grip without stickiness. The rest positions your thumb naturally for accessing the thumb buttons and horizontal scroll wheel without stretching or awkward movements.

Weight and Balance

At 141 grams, the MX Master 3S is substantial but not heavy. The weight feels premium and provides stability during use, preventing the light, cheap feeling of budget mice. The balance is carefully engineered, distributing weight to enhance control rather than causing fatigue.

Material Quality

The combination of smooth plastic body, textured rubber grips, and precision metal scroll wheel creates a tactile experience that feels expensive and refined. The materials are selected not just for aesthetics but for functional benefits â€“ the rubber provides grip where needed, while smooth surfaces reduce friction for easy gliding.

Multi-Device Connectivity

One of the MX Master 3S's most powerful productivity features is its ability to connect to and control up to three devices simultaneously, with instant switching between them.

Easy-Switch Technology

Three buttons on the mouse's underside correspond to three device connections. Press a button to instantly switch control to that device. The transition is immediate â€“ no lag, no reconnection delay, just instant control of the selected device.

For users working with multiple computers (e.g., work laptop, personal desktop, home server), this feature eliminates the frustration of constantly pairing/unpairing or maintaining multiple mice. Keep all devices connected and switch between them as needed throughout your workflow.

Logi Flow Technology

The revolutionary Logi Flow feature enables seamless cursor movement between computers and even text/image copy-paste across devices. Position your computers' displays in Logi Options+ software to match physical arrangement, then move your cursor off one screen edge to appear on the adjacent computer's screen.

This cross-computer workflow is transformative for users managing multiple systems. Copy text or images on one computer, move the cursor to another computer, and paste â€“ the data transfers automatically. It feels like magic the first time you experience it, then quickly becomes indispensable.

Dual Connectivity: Bluetooth and USB

The MX Master 3S supports both Bluetooth and Logitech's Logi Bolt USB receiver connectivity. Bluetooth provides convenient connection without occupying USB ports, while Logi Bolt offers lower latency and more reliable connection in interference-heavy environments.

The flexibility to use either connection method based on device capabilities and environment ensures compatibility across virtually any computer. The mouse remembers connections, making it easy to maintain three devices connected via a mix of Bluetooth and USB receiver.

Battery Life and Charging

The integrated rechargeable battery delivers exceptional longevity, eliminating the waste and inconvenience of disposable batteries.

70-Day Battery Life

Logitech claims up to 70 days of use on a full charge â€“ a realistic estimate for typical office use. Heavy users might see 30-40 days, while lighter users could exceed the 70-day estimate. Regardless of usage patterns, the battery life is long enough that charging becomes a monthly task rather than a weekly concern.

Fast Charging

A USB-C charging port (a welcome upgrade from the MX Master 3's micro-USB) enables fast charging. A quick 3-minute charge provides a full day of use, while a full charge from empty takes about 2 hours. The USB-C standardization also means one less cable type to maintain.

The mouse remains fully functional while charging, so even if you forget to charge overnight, a quick lunch-break charge session keeps you productive. Battery status is displayed in Logi Options+ software and through the mouse's indicator light.

Customization Through Logi Options+

The comprehensive Logi Options+ software (replacing the older Logitech Options) unlocks the MX Master 3S's full potential through extensive customization.

Button Customization

All seven buttons are programmable, allowing assignment of custom functions, keyboard shortcuts, or specific commands. Create application-specific profiles that automatically load when switching software â€“ for example, configure buttons for layer navigation in Photoshop, different shortcuts in Excel, and media controls in your video player.

The customization depth is impressive without being overwhelming. Common functions are easily accessible, while power users can dive deep into advanced macro creation and multi-step command sequences.

Application-Specific Settings

Beyond button customization, you can configure scroll speed, pointer speed, and scroll direction on per-application basis. If you prefer natural scrolling in some apps but traditional scrolling in others, Logi Options+ accommodates your preferences automatically.

Gesture Controls

The thumb button can trigger customizable gesture controls, enabling quick access to application switcher, virtual desktops, volume controls, or custom actions. These gestures feel natural once learned, providing quick access to common functions without keyboard shortcuts.

Comparison to Alternatives

vs. Logitech MX Master 3

The MX Master 3 costs slightly less but lacks silent clicks and the upgraded 8K DPI sensor. If quiet operation matters, the 3S is worth the premium. If you're on a tight budget and don't mind normal click noise, the MX Master 3 remains excellent.

vs. Razer Pro Click Mini

Razer's productivity mouse is smaller and slightly lighter, better for travel but less comfortable for extended desktop use. It lacks the MagSpeed wheel and horizontal scroll. Consider the Razer for mobile professionals prioritizing portability; the MX Master 3S for desktop-focused productivity.

vs. Microsoft Surface Precision Mouse

Microsoft's alternative offers similar multi-device connectivity at lower cost but lacks the MagSpeed wheel, silent clicks, and sophisticated Logi Options+ software. The Surface mouse works but doesn't match the refined experience of the MX Master 3S.
