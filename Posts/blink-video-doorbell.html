Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a subscription to use the Blink Video Doorbell?

No subscription is required for basic functionality including live view, motion alerts, and two-way audio. However, cloud storage of recorded clips requires a Blink Subscription Plan ($3/month) or you can use local storage with the optional Sync Module 2.

Can I use the Blink Video Doorbell without WiFi?

No, the doorbell requires a 2.4 GHz WiFi connection to function. It cannot operate offline or connect to 5 GHz networks.

Will the Blink Video Doorbell work with my existing doorbell chime?

The battery-powered Blink Video Doorbell does not ring existing mechanical or electronic chimes. You'll receive notifications on your smartphone and can use Echo devices as chimes through Alexa announcements.

How long do clips record for?

Clip length is adjustable from 5 to 60 seconds. Longer clips capture more context but use more battery and storage. Default is 30 seconds.

Can I view my doorbell camera on my computer?

Currently, the Blink Video Doorbell can only be viewed through the mobile app (iOS/Android), Echo Show devices, or Fire TV devices. There is no web browser interface.
