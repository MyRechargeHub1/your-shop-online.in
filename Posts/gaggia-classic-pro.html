Daily Routine

A typical morning workflow: Turn on the machine and wait 10-15 minutes for full temperature stabilization. Flush water briefly to stabilize temperature. Start extraction and watch the flowâ€”aim for 25-30 seconds to yield about 36-40 grams of espresso. Stop extraction when it reaches blonde color or desired volume.
