World-Class Active Noise Cancellation

Bose pioneered consumer noise-cancelling headphones decades ago, and the QuietComfort Ultra demonstrates why they remain the gold standard. The sophisticated ANC system uses advanced algorithms and precision-tuned microphones to eliminate environmental noise with effectiveness that continues to lead the industry.

The system employs six external microphones continuously monitoring ambient sound, analyzing noise patterns thousands of times per second, and generating precise anti-noise waveforms that destructively interfere with unwanted sound. This real-time processing adapts to changing noise environments instantly, maintaining optimal cancellation whether you're on an airplane, subway, or busy office.

Quiet Mode Excellence

Engage Quiet Mode, and the world simply disappears. The dramatic noise reduction creates a cocoon of silence that transforms noisy environments into peaceful sanctuaries. Airplane engine rumble becomes barely noticeable. Subway screeching fades to whispers. Office chatter disappears entirely.

Aware Mode

When situational awareness is needed, Aware Mode uses the same microphone array to pipe ambient sound through transparently. Hear announcements, conversations, and environmental sounds naturally without removing the headphones. The transparency is so natural you forget you're wearing headphones at all.

Aware Mode with ActiveSense automatically reduces sudden loud sounds while maintaining awareness of important environmental audio. A car horn remains audible but isn't startlingly loud; construction noise is managed without complete isolation. This intelligent awareness proves ideal for walking, commuting, or working environments requiring occasional interaction.

Revolutionary Immersive Audio

The QuietComfort Ultra introduces Bose's Immersive Audio â€“ a spatial audio technology that creates three-dimensional soundstages far beyond traditional stereo. Motion-Tracked Spatial Audio

Built-in motion sensors track your head movement, anchoring audio sources in space even as you turn your head. Watch a movie and turn right â€“ the dialog remains centered on the screen while ambient sounds shift naturally. The spatial audio works with any content, not just specially-encoded Dolby Atmos tracks. Bose's proprietary processing analyzes stereo recordings and intelligently expands them into immersive spatial presentations. Your entire music library gains new dimension and depth.

Still and Motion Modes

Immersive Audio offers two modes: Motion mode with head-tracking for video content, and Still mode that creates a fixed spatial soundstage for music listening. Still mode provides the expanded soundstage without head-tracking, perfect for focused music appreciation where you're not watching video content.

CustomTune Technology

Personalized Sound Optimization

During initial setup, the headphones emit test tones while microphones measure how sound reflects within your ear canals. The headphones then create a personalized EQ profile optimizing frequency response, ANC effectiveness, and spatial audio rendering specifically for your ears.

The difference proves immediately noticeable. Bass extends deeper, mids become clearer, and highs sparkle with detail. The ANC performs more effectively because it accounts for your ear seal and anatomy. It's like having headphones custom-tuned by an audio engineer specifically for your ears.
