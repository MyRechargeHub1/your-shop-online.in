Why Blunt Blades Work Better

This seems counterintuitive, but Blendtec's blunt blade design is actually superior to sharp blades for most blending tasks. Sharp blades slice through ingredients, which can leave chunks and create uneven textures. Blunt blades driven at extremely high speed by a powerful motor literally pulverize ingredients at the molecular level, creating silky-smooth textures impossible with conventional blenders. The blunt edges also last forever—they never dull because they aren't sharp to begin with.

Tips for Getting the Most from Your Designer 725

Maximize your blender's potential with these techniques:

Loading Order Matters

  • Always add liquids first (water, juice, milk) to create vortex
  • Add soft ingredients next (banana, yogurt, fresh fruit)
  • Top with frozen ingredients and ice last
  • This sequence ensures proper vortex formation and smooth blending

Cycle Selection

  • Trust the pre-programmed cycles—they're optimized through extensive testing
  • Smoothie cycle works for most fruit/vegetable blends
  • Hot soup cycle needs full jar for proper friction heating
  • Ice cream cycle requires frozen fruit only—no added liquid

Cleaning

  • Clean immediately after use for easiest cleanup
  • Self-cleaning cycle: Add 2 cups warm water + drop of dish soap, run 45-second cycle
  • Rinse thoroughly and air dry
  • For stubborn residue, soak jar in warm soapy water before cleaning cycle
  • Jar and lid are dishwasher safe on top rack
