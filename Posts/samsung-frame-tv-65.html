Stunning QLED Picture Quality

The Frame features Samsung's Quantum Dot technology delivering over a billion colors with exceptional accuracy and vibrancy. The 4K resolution (3840 x 2160) provides stunning detail whether watching movies, sports, or gaming. Quantum Dots are nano-sized particles that emit precisely controlled colors when hit by light, producing purer, more accurate colors than traditional LED TVs.

The result is a picture that looks incredibly lifelike with vibrant yet natural colors. Reds are rich without oversaturation, blues are deep without looking artificial, greens have nuanced variation rather than cartoon-like flatness. Skin tones look realistic across diverse ethnicities and lighting conditionsâ€”critical for movie watching and photo display.

Exceptional Brightness and HDR

Peak brightness reaches impressive levels, ensuring excellent visibility even in bright rooms with windows. HDR (High Dynamic Range) support includes HDR10, HDR10+, and HLG formats, displaying content with expanded contrast and color range. Bright highlights shine without blowing out detail, while dark shadows retain visible information rather than becoming muddy black voids.

HDR10+ provides scene-by-scene optimization, adjusting brightness and contrast for each frame rather than applying static settings to entire movies. This dynamic optimization ensures every scene looks its best whether it's a dark dungeon, bright desert, or transitional twilight. The technology extracts maximum visual impact from compatible content.

Revolutionary Art Mode

Art Mode transforms The Frame from television into artwork when not actively watching content. The integrated motion sensor detects room occupancy, activating Art Mode when people are present and turning off the display when the room is empty to conserve energy. The transition between TV and art modes is seamless and automatic.

Gaming Performance

The Frame supports 4K gaming at 120Hz through HDMI 2.1, ideal for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X. Game Mode optimizes picture settings for responsive gameplay while maintaining good image quality.

Game Bar and Features

Energy-Efficient Operation
